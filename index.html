How ScholarPass Works

ScholarPass is a digital platform that helps students easily discover, access, and apply for scholarships, making opportunities more accessible and hassle-free.

Search for College Scholarships

Discover scholarships designed for you! Create your profile, and our system will instantly search thousands of programs to connect you with the best opportunities, helping you save time and boost your chances of success.

About ScholarPass

ScholarPass is committed to empowering students by bridging the gap between learners and scholarship providers. Our mission is to make education accessible, affordable, and inclusive by helping students discover the right opportunities and guiding providers to reach the right candidates.

ScholarPass produces personalized scholarship recommendations for every student using our AI-powered matching system, considering their interests, background, and academic history.

ScholarPass ensures that all students, regardless of their financial situation, have the same opportunities to continue their education by making it easier to locate and apply.

ScholarPass is a proposed web-based application designed to support SDG 4: Quality Education by breaking down barriers to access, targeting tertiary-level students from families with limited resources.
